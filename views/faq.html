Your Questions , Our Answers ...

We understand that it takes remarkable courage to open up about your problems. Good on you for taking the first step. Here's some of the most frequently asked questions on our platform.

How accurate are the results from the app? +
We are constantly working on improving the app's performance. The metrics derived from the app are based off the responses gathered in the publicly known 'DiagnoseMe' dataset that contains doctor-patient interactions.
How do I use this app? +
You can create an account on our platform and take our quiz that analyses your mental health. We also provide you with a take home report for review later on. If you do run into any questions, please get in touch with us through our contact page.
I have questions regarding my report results? +
Please get in touch with us through our contact section and one of our members will review your request and get back to you at the earliest.
